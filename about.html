ABOUT US

La Vita Living Products is a multi-level sales company that supports indepenedent distributors engaged in selling our products.Distributors are encouraged to build networks of distributors and expand their businesses.We offer training sessions to give our distributors the necessary skills for starting their own businesses and work according to a Profit Plan that encourages members to always aim higher.

OUR HISTORY

La Vita was established in 1995.From the humble beginnings of the first branch in Sunny Park,Pretoria,the company has now expanded to nine branches nationwide.The branches are spread across Gauteng,North West,Limpopo,Kwa Zulu Natal and the Free State

OUR LOGO

The La Vita Living Products logo is of a seagull to new flying heights and was inspired by the book johnathan Livingston Seagull by Richard Bach.The book is about a seagull with dreams bigger than the rest of his flock.
